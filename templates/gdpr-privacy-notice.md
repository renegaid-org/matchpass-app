# Privacy Notice — MatchPass at [Club Name]

## What We Process
When you present your Signet credential at the gate, MatchPass processes:
- Your Signet public key (a pseudonym — not your name)
- Your photo (fetched from Blossom, displayed for 2 seconds, then discarded)
- Your card and ban status

## Why
To keep everyone safe at [Club Name] matches. We check that you are not currently banned or suspended.

## How Long We Keep It
- Scan records: 30 days after the match, then deleted.
- Cards (yellows, reds): until they expire via clean sheet, then deleted at season end.
- Bans: until the ban expires, then deleted.

## Your Rights
- You can challenge any card within 48 hours (yellow) or 7 days (red).
- You can request a copy of your data by contacting [Club contact].
- You can request deletion of your data, subject to safety obligations.

## Who Controls Your Data
[Club Name] is the data controller. MatchPass provides the technology. Your club controls your data.
